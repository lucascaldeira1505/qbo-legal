Privacy Policy

QBO Classifier  ·  Last updated: March 2026

QBO Classifier ("the App") is an internal tool used solely by its owner to classify QuickBooks Online transactions. This policy explains how data is handled.

1. Data Collected

The App accesses QuickBooks Online data (transactions, accounts, classes, vendors) through the Intuit API solely to perform transaction classification. No data is collected from third parties or end users.

2. How Data Is Used

  • Transaction data is stored locally in a SQLite database on the owner's machine.
  • Data is used exclusively to suggest account classifications and sync summaries to Notion.
  • Data is never sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party beyond the integrations configured by the owner (Notion, Slack).

3. Data Retention

Data is retained locally as long as the owner chooses. It can be deleted at any time by removing the local database file.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with:

  • Intuit QuickBooks Online — to read and write transaction data.
  • Notion — to display transaction summaries.
  • Slack — to send daily notification summaries.

Each service's own privacy policy governs data processed on their platforms.

5. Security

OAuth 2.0 tokens are stored locally in a .env file accessible only to the owner. No credentials are transmitted beyond the official Intuit OAuth endpoints.

6. Contact

For any questions, contact the owner at the email associated with this GitHub account.
